Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At JoyJunction, we prioritize your privacy. Our services process the following types of information:

  • Images: When you use our Critter Twin Finder or Sentiment Scope, we process the photos you upload.
  • Generated Passwords: When you use our Password Generator tool.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with our services.

2. How We Process Your Information

  • Photo Processing: All photo analysis is performed directly in your browser. Your photos are never stored on our servers.
  • Password Generation: Passwords are generated locally in your browser and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. This data is anonymized.

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security of your information through the following measures:

  • All processing is done client-side in your browser
  • We do not maintain any databases of user information
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • We regularly update our security measures

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Request information about our data practices
